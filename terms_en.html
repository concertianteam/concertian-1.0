Entrance ticket/Ticket

A valid ticket is a valuable document and certificate, which allows the ticket owner to enter the event, which ticket was bought for. As the owner based on owner ticket and after proving your identity as an owner of entrance ticket, you are allowed for one-time enter. After that, the purpose of the ticket is fulfilled, and it is not possible to apply it again. For instance after leaving the place where performance takes place before the end of concert, you are not allowed to enter it again based on ticket, which was already used.

Tickets may not be offered for resale, return to sale, exchange or duplicated. Any attempt to reproduce the ticket, by making duplicates of it, is forbidden and punishable.

The ticket is only valid in case, when identity and purchase data coincide, making it applicable just once and for event, which was purchased for.

Entrance ticket serves as a valid certificate for one time entry (limited to date and time of the event it was bought for – also written on its cover).

Actual prices and available tickets are listed on concertian.com, where they can be also purchased under the terms and conditions of this document.

Ticket also stands as a receipt or proof of payment for selected event and it gives a right to owner to enter and remain in the area, where event takes place. Ticket contains the following information, therefore we do not recommend any further spreading:

a) name of the event or name of performers,
b) entry price (price of the ticket) at the time of buying it,
c) place, date and time of concert,
d) an unique identifier consist of 6 numbers.

Every visitor has a right to buy just limited number of tickets, unless it is closer specified by event. Quawer s.r.o. reserves the right to limit the amount of tickets sold to visitors based on its decision.
